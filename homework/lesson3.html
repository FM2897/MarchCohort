Homemade Hot Chocolate

We all know that it's freezing outside this winter so why not get cozy indoors by enjoying this hommade hot chocolate to keep you warm as you unwind and relieve stress from the day

Here are the 6 ingredients you'll need

  • Whole Milk (Optional)
  • Cocoa powder
  • Dark Chocolate Chips
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Salt

How to Make Hot Chocolate

  1. Place the milk of your choice in a saucepan over medium-low heat. Using milk instead of water, makes this hot chocolate extra creamy and flavorful.
  2. Whisk in cocoa powder and sugar, and heat until warm.
  3. Once the milk is warm, add your chocolate chips and stir for 3 to 5 minutes, or until the chocolate is fully melted and combined.
  4. Add a splash of vanilla extract.
  5. Serve immediately, topped with your favorite garnishes: marshmallows, whipped cream, chopped chocolate, crushed candy canes or more.

Do enjoy your lovely homemade cup of hot chocolate that you made!
